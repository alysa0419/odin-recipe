Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground meat (½ pound ground pork, ½ pound lean ground beef).
  • 1 onion (diced)
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • Lasagna noodles
  • 1 cup of cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
